Common questions

Everything you need to know

🛡️Is the content safe for my child?
Yes — and we take this seriously. Every story is generated by an AI model that we've specifically instructed to produce G-rated, family-friendly content only. Before any story is written, we automatically screen your inputs (names, interests, locations) for anything unsuitable. The story generator itself is given strict rules: if anything looks out of place, it silently substitutes it with something wholesome — so you always get a safe, warm story. Our platform is built for families with children aged 2–12, and every decision we make reflects that.
🎂What ages are the stories suitable for?
Stories are calibrated to each child's age. Ages 2–4 get short, repetitive, sensory-rich sentences with a safe and magical world. Ages 5–7 get slightly more plot and a little gentle challenge. Ages 8–10 get richer vocabulary and real emotional depth. And for older kids (11+), the prose feels closer to chapter-book quality. If you have children of different ages, each story is tailored individually to whoever it stars.
⚡How quickly will I get my stories?
Usually under 5 minutes from the moment you complete payment. Stories generate in the background while you watch a live progress feed. Once all 10 are ready, a magic link is emailed to you instantly — no account needed, just click to listen.
🎁Can I give these as a gift?
Absolutely — gifting is a big part of what we do. When you purchase as a gift, we create a beautiful personalised gift reveal page that the recipient opens themselves. You can include the child's name so the page greets them personally. The gift link is included in your confirmation email to share however you like — text, email, printed card. See how gifting works →
🔗How do I access my stories later?
Your stories are saved for 12 months from purchase. Every time you want to listen, just use the magic link in your confirmation email — no login, no password. We strongly recommend saving that email. You can also download each story as an mp3 so it's yours forever, even after the link expires.
✏️Can I customise what goes into the stories?
More than you might expect. You tell us your child's name, age, what they love, favourite Sunshine Coast locations, and the vibe you're after. You can also add friends, cousins, or siblings as characters. Before payment, we show you a full Story DNA preview — and you can tap any location, activity, narrative arc, or character to swap it before a single word is written.
📻Can I use these on a Yoto player?
Yes — and it works brilliantly. Once your stories are generated, use the Download All MP3s button to save all 10 stories at once. Then upload them to your Yoto player using Yoto's Make Your Own card feature at yotoplay.com/make-your-own. Your child gets fully screen-free, on-demand access to their own personalised stories — completely independently.
🔒What do you do with my information?
The details you enter (names, interests, locations) are used only to generate your stories and are stored securely. We don't sell, share, or use them for marketing. Payment is handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card details. You can read the full picture in our Privacy Policy.
🎙️What does the narration sound like?
Each story is narrated by a warm Australian voice using ElevenLabs, one of the world's leading AI audio platforms. The voice is expressive but consistent — not robotic, not over-the-top. We've tuned the settings specifically to keep the tone steady and soothing for children. You can preview what it sounds like on the sample stories page.
