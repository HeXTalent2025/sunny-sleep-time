Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

Sunny Stories is operated by Jeff Teale, trading as Sunny Stories, Sunshine Coast, Queensland, Australia. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

What information we collect

To create your personalised stories, we ask you to provide:

  • Your child or children's first names
  • Their approximate ages
  • Favourite Sunshine Coast locations
  • Your email address (to deliver your stories)

We also receive your payment confirmation from Stripe, including a transaction ID. We do not store or handle your payment card details — these are processed entirely by Stripe.

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Generate your personalised story collection
  • Email you a link to access your stories
  • Allow you to revisit your stories via a secure magic link

We do not use your information for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service you paid for.

AI story generation

Your child's name, age, and chosen locations are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your stories. Anthropic processes this data on our behalf and does not use it to train their models. For more information, see Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

How long we keep your data

Your stories are stored securely in our database for up to 12 months from the date of purchase, giving you ongoing access via your magic link. After this period, your stories and associated personal information are automatically deleted.

Your email address is retained only as needed to provide the service and is not added to any mailing list.

Who we share data with

We use the following third-party services to operate Sunny Stories:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic — AI story generation (Anthropic Privacy Policy)
  • Upstash — secure data storage
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — website hosting

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

Security

Your stories are accessed via a unique, unguessable token link. We do not require passwords. Data is stored encrypted at rest and transmitted over HTTPS.

Accessing or deleting your information

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. To make a request, email us at hello@sunnystories.co. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's privacy

Sunny Stories is a service for parents and guardians creating stories for their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. By purchasing, you confirm that you are the parent or guardian of the children named in the stories.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be noted on this page with an updated date.
